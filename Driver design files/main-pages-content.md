The occupational scale for a Commercial Driver average s $2,400 - $3,600 monthly. The cost of the Commercial Driver Training/Class “A” CDL is $4500.00. Price Includes cost of training, books, supplies, DOT medical physical, and drug screen. This truck driving programs teach a person the skills and knowledge needed to drive a Class-A commercial vehicle and to get their Class-A CDL License. Tri-County Driving Academy strives to make sure you successfully obtain your CDL "A " License. Preparation for the Class-A Commercial Written Exams Class-A Commercial Road Test Pre-trip Inspection Backing Maneuvers Coupling and Uncoupling Defensive Driving and Safety DOT Physical included Tri-County Driving Academy helps place its graduates for employment with several major carriers. Many of our graduates have completed employment applications, conducted any necessary interviews, and selected with whom they would like to drive before graduation. This process allows all students the ability to go to work immediately after completion of training. The training program is four weeks. First week is preparation for the Class “A” commercial drivers learners permit. Also you will be scheduled during class hours to complete your DOT physical, the price of the physical is included in the total cost of the program. The remainder three weeks you will receive hands-on training in the trucks. DMV testing will be completed during the last week of class. Admission Requirement: Applicant must have a valid Driver’s License, provide a DMV driving record, and pass a DOT physical. Class begins every three weeks call for scheduling. View on Mobile
