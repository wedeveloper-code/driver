Train on the Best

Our Training Fleet & Facilities

Your career demands real-world experience. We maintain a diverse, modern fleet of manual and automatic semi-trucks, heavy equipment, and a fully operational mechanics bay to ensure you are day-one ready.

No "E" Restrictions

We train and test on 10-speed manual transmissions so you graduate with a full, unrestricted Class A CDL, maximizing your job opportunities.

Modern Automatics

With 80% of major fleets moving to automated manual transmissions (AMTs), we ensure you get seat time in the exact trucks you'll drive professionally.

DOT Compliant

Every piece of equipment is meticulously maintained by our in-house diesel mechanics program to exceed FMCSA and DOT safety standards.

Commercial Trucks & Trailers

Our Trailer Fleet

We don't just teach you to drive the truck; we teach you how to handle the load. Our yard features a mix of 53' Dry Vans (both empty and loaded with water-weights for realistic simulation) and 48' Flatbeds for load securement training.

Diesel Mechanics Facility

State-of-the-Art Repair Bays

Our 10,000 sq. ft. maintenance facility serves two purposes: it keeps our CDL fleet in pristine, DOT-compliant condition, and it serves as the hands-on classroom for our Diesel Mechanics students.

  • Full-Service Pits & Lifts Three fully equipped bays capable of lifting fully loaded tractor-trailers for undercarriage inspection and repair.
  • Advanced Diagnostics Lab Equipped with Cummins INSITE™, Detroit Diagnostic Link, and Bendix ACom software to read and clear ECM fault codes.
  • HVAC & Brake Stations Dedicated stations for air brake board simulations and Freon recovery/recharging.
