Program Overview

Tri-County Driving Academy's Commercial Driver/Class "A" CDL Training program will provide you with the knowledge and skills to become a Class "A" licensed driver qualifying you to drive long distances, regionally or for a local carrier.

The occupational scale for a Commercial Driver averages $2,400–$3,600 monthly. The cost of the Commercial Driver Training/Class "A" CDL is $4,500.00. Price includes cost of training, books, supplies, DOT medical physical, and drug screen.

This truck driving program teaches a person the skills and knowledge needed to drive a Class-A commercial vehicle and to get their Class-A CDL License. Tri-County Driving Academy strives to make sure you successfully obtain your CDL "A" License.

What You'll Learn

Preparation for Class-A Commercial Written Exams
Class-A Commercial Road Test
Pre-Trip Inspection
Backing Maneuvers
Coupling and Uncoupling
Defensive Driving and Safety
DOT Physical Included
Job Placement Assistance

Career Opportunities

Tri-County Driving Academy helps place its graduates for employment with several major carriers. Many of our graduates have completed employment applications, conducted any necessary interviews, and selected with whom they would like to drive before completing the program.
