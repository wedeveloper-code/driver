Financial Assistance Options

You may qualify for funding that covers your entire tuition. Our staff will help you explore every available option.

WIOA Funding

Workforce Innovation & Opportunity Act

WIOA grants are available for eligible students who meet income and employment requirements. This funding can cover tuition, books, and supplies for approved programs. Contact your local Workforce Development office or ask our staff for help applying.

Federal Pell Grant

No Repayment Required

Federal Pell Grants may be available to qualifying students based on financial need. Unlike loans, Pell Grants do not need to be repaid. The amount varies based on your financial situation, enrollment status, and cost of attendance.

Veterans Benefits

VA Education Benefits

If you are a military veteran, VA education benefits may cover your training costs. We work with veterans to ensure they receive the benefits they have earned. Contact us to learn more about eligibility and the application process.

Payment Plans

Flexible Options

We understand that everyone's financial situation is different. Our staff will work with you to find a payment arrangement that fits your budget. Don't let finances prevent you from starting the career you deserve.
