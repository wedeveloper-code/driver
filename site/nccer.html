About NCCER Training

Tri-County Driving Academy is now offering NCCER Training in Heavy Equipment Operations Level One. Students successfully completing Heavy Equipment Operations Level One will earn industry-recognized credentials verifiable through the NCCER Automated National Registry.

NCCER (National Center for Construction Education and Research) provides standardized construction and maintenance curricula that are recognized by thousands of employers across the United States. When you complete an NCCER program, your credentials are stored in the national registry, making it easy for employers to verify your qualifications.

The construction industry is in constant need of skilled heavy equipment operators. NCCER certification distinguishes you from other candidates and demonstrates that you've met national standards for safety and competency.
