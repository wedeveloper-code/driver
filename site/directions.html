From Interstate 81

  1. 1

    Take Virginia Exit 14 (Abingdon) just north of Bristol. Turn right at the end of the exit. (If coming north from Tennessee, the exit automatically goes to the right.)

  2. 2

    Go to the second red light and turn right onto Lee Hwy / US 11. This is a "T" intersection and you will have to turn right or left.

  3. 3

    Go to the second red light and turn left onto US19 N / Alt. 58 W. You will see Walgreens on your right and CVS (Rite Aid) on your left at this light.

  4. 4

    Go approximately 12 miles until you enter Russell County. You will pass a Dodge Dealership on your right, go about ½ to 1 mile and you will see a Valero gas station on your right. This is Hansonville.

  5. 5

    Turn right into the parking lot of the Valero gas station, go to the back of the parking lot and you will see our driveway that turns down the hill. We are the last blue building on the hill.
