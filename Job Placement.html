Career Services

How We Support Your Job Search

Finding a job can be overwhelming, especially in a new industry. Our placement staff acts as a bridge between you and the trucking companies, ensuring your application stands out and you find the carrier that best fits your lifestyle goals.

Lifetime Assistance

Once you graduate from Tri-County Driving Academy, you are part of the family. Whether it's your first job out of school or you're looking to switch companies 5 years later, our doors are always open to alumni.

1. Pre-Hire Letters

We help you submit applications before you even start your training. Many companies offer "Pre-Hire Letters" — a conditional job offer that says they will hire you as soon as you pass your CDL exam. This gives you peace of mind while you study.

2. Resume & Application Building

Trucking applications require specific details about your 10-year employment history and MVR. We assist you in building a professional driver's resume and filling out DOT-compliant applications correctly the first time.

3. On-Site Recruiter Visits

Every week, recruiters from national and regional carriers visit our classrooms. You will have the opportunity to hear their presentations, ask questions about home time and pay structure, and conduct face-to-face interviews on the spot.

4. Interview Prep & Evaluation

Not sure what a dispatcher or safety manager will ask you? We conduct mock interviews to prepare you for questions about safety protocols, logbooks, and scenarios you might face on the road.
