Admissions & Funding

Don't Let Cost Stop You From a Great Career

At Tri-County Driving Academy, we believe everyone deserves a chance to build a solid career. We offer multiple funding options to ensure tuition isn't a barrier.

  • WIOA Grants

    We are a WIOA (Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act) approved training provider. You may qualify for full tuition coverage.

  • Veterans Assistance

    Proudly accepting GI Bill® and other military educational benefits.

  • Flexible Payment Plans

    Affordable weekly/monthly installment plans available for out-of-pocket students.
