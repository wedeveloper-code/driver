State Grants (WIOA)

The Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act (WIOA) is a federal program that provides funding for individuals who need training to enter or re-enter the workforce. If you qualify, WIOA can cover up to 100% of your CDL training tuition.

Who may qualify?

  • Dislocated workers (recently laid off or receiving unemployment).
  • Low-income individuals looking to improve their earning potential.
  • Underemployed individuals currently working part-time.

How to apply: Our admissions staff works directly with local Workforce Development boards. We will guide you through the application process, provide the necessary school documentation, and help you secure your training voucher.

Military & Veterans Benefits

Tri-County Driving Academy is proud to support our active-duty military, reservists, and veterans. We are approved to accept various VA educational benefits for our commercial driving programs.

Post-9/11 GI Bill®

Eligible veterans can use Chapter 33 benefits to cover the cost of tuition, and may also qualify for a monthly housing allowance while attending full-time training.

VR&E (Chapter 31)

The Veteran Readiness and Employment program provides services to eligible service members and veterans with service-connected disabilities to help them prepare for, find, and maintain suitable careers.

Montgomery GI Bill®

Active Duty (Chapter 30) and Selected Reserve (Chapter 1606) benefits can be applied toward our CDL training programs. Let our certifying official assist you with the paperwork.

*GI Bill® is a registered trademark of the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs (VA). More information about education benefits offered by VA is available at the official U.S. government Web site at https://www.benefits.va.gov/gibill.

Alternative Financing Options

If you don't qualify for state or federal grants, we offer several flexible ways to fund your CDL education.

In-House Payment Plans

Pay as you go! We offer interest-free, short-term installment plans directly through the school.

  • Requires an initial down payment.
  • Weekly payments during training.
  • Zero interest charges.
  • No credit check required.

Partner Lenders

We partner with leading financial institutions that specialize in career training and trade school loans.

  • Fixed or variable interest rates.
  • Options to defer payments until after graduation.
  • Co-signer options available.
Most Popular Route

Employer Tuition Reimbursement

Many major trucking companies offer tuition reimbursement programs (up to $7,000+) as a sign-on incentive.

You pay for your schooling upfront (or via loan), and once you are hired, your new employer pays you back in monthly installments on top of your regular paycheck! Our placement team will connect you with carriers offering these programs.
